Indoor/Outdoor 360° Virtual Tour Made Easy

Virtual tour creation has never been easier. Based on a powerful V-SLAM algorithm and multiple visual sensors, Pilot One can pinpoint indoor or outdoor positions to a centimeter-level. Now get rid of the paid platforms and slow processing. Use Pilot One to generate a well-organized virtual tour automatically and share it directly to your social media or embed it on your website instantly.

Swiftbird 8K Cloud Live
Streaming Solution

Say goodbye to tedious 8K live streaming. Labpano innovated the cloud live streaming solution, which allows you to stream 8K videos via ethernet, Wi-Fi, and 5G cellular network to multiple destinations with one second of latency. The solution integrates high-end hardware and software, a license for 8K streaming, and a data package. Simultaneous live-streaming and recording are supported.

Easy Street View Capture

Pilot One is the most efficient 8K 360° camera for street view photography. With the support of an ultra-precision GPS, a powerful processor, and real-time stitching, it generates premium 8K images up to a speed of 7fps and allows you to directly upload from the camera, making it unsurpassed in terms of performance, ease-of-use, and portability.

PilotSteady™️ Stabilization

Pilot One is the world's first 360° camera that performs real-time stabilization while live streaming 360° VR. Patented PilotSteady™️ stabilization with our intelligent shake compensation algorithm and a built-in 9-axis gyroscope achieve real-time correction and automatic compensation of video images.

True 8K Imaging Capability

Pilot One adopts 4 sets of Sony 1/2.3" 12MP sensors and ultra-high precision optical lenses, our initiated android-based Pilot OS and a set of AI algorithms. You can manually adjust EV, ISO, stitching distance, and other parameters freely, or utilize our HDR mode for better results easily.

Smart Touch Screen 2.0

Labpano brought in a revolutionary smart touch screen on its first flagship device – Pilot Era, which made it possible for the first time to shoot, edit, and share professional 360° content without the requirement of a smartphone or a PC. Following our initiative, we now release Smart Touch Screen 2.0 on Pilot One, bringing you a lighter, smoother, and faster operating experience.
